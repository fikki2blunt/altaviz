About Altaviz Support

Leading ATM service provider with over 10 years of industry experience

Your Trusted ATM Partner

Since 2014, Altaviz Support has been the premier ATM service provider in Nigeria, establishing ourselves as the number one partner of GRG Banking while expanding our expertise across all major ATM manufacturers. We specialize in comprehensive ATM solutions including sales, installation, repair, and ongoing maintenance services. Our team of certified technicians brings extensive experience working with all major ATM brands including NCR and Wincor (now Diebold) Nixdorf.

We understand that ATM downtime directly impacts your revenue. That's why we've built our reputation on rapid response times, quality workmanship, and exceptional customer service. As the leading GRG Banking partner in Nigeria, we maintain the largest inventory of genuine parts and have unmatched expertise in GRG systems.

With our strategic partnership with GRG Banking and deep knowledge of all ATM brands, we provide unparalleled service quality. Our emergency response team and nationwide network of technicians ensure that help is always available when you need it most.
